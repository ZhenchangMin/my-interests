Ch. 01 — On the Stage

A pop song built like a building.

K-Pop is engineering you can dance to. Replace the next paragraphs with your own — the group you stan, the company you'd argue about, the comeback that rewired your week.

Write about your entry point. The fancam that found you, the friend who insisted, the algorithm that finally understood.

Ch. 02 — The Group

The line-up I'd defend in any group chat.

Name the group. One sentence on their sound. Pick a member and explain the part of the choreography only they could pull off. Be specific — the second they break formation, the look right before the chorus.

Bonus: list two B-sides better than the title track. Justify briefly.

Ch. 03 — One Song

The track on permanent rotation.

One song, one verse. Describe the production choice that does the most work — the drop, the key change, the silence before the final chorus. Tell me where you were when it clicked.

End with the one second of choreography you keep miming when no one's looking.
